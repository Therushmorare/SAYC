Organizational Structure

SAYC‚Äôs structure is designed to ensure effective governance, inclusive representation, and impactful youth development across all levels of society.

01

National Executive Committee (NEC)

The highest decision-making body responsible for overall strategic direction, comprising elected youth leaders from regions across South Africa.

02

Provincial Executive Committees (PEC)

Comprised of 15 elected leaders per province, advocating for youth matters and overseeing the implementation of provincial initiatives.

03

Regional Executive Committees (REC)

Ten elected leaders representing districts, ensuring youth issues are addressed and regional programmes are effectively implemented.

04

Branches Executive Committee (BEC)

The grassroots level of SAYC operating at local municipality level, serving as the direct link between SAYC and communities.

Types of Organizations Affiliated to SAYC

Service-Based Organizations

Provide direct support and services addressing economic and social needs of youth. Includes youth-owned entities, NPOs, NPCs, and private companies fostering entrepreneurship and economic empowerment.

Issue-Based Organizations

Operate under constitutional rights to freedom of expression and assembly, advocating for youth-related social change through NGOs, NPOs, and CBOs.

Membership-Based Organizations

Enable collective participation through political youth formations, student representative bodies, and democratic membership structures.
